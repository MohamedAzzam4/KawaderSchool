Our Story

A Journey of Excellence in Technical Nursing Education

Kawader Secondary Technical School for Nursing in Beheira was established in response to the growing need for highly qualified medical personnel in the Egyptian healthcare sector.

Our school is an outstanding alternative to traditional high school, where students receive intensive training combining theoretical academics and practical application in the best certified governmental and private hospitals in the governorate, preparing them to enter the job market immediately upon graduation.

Vision

To be the leading school in graduating distinguished nursing personnel nationwide, recognized for quality education and clinical training, contributing to raising the standard of healthcare.

Mission

To qualify scientifically and practically equipped nurses to provide high-quality health services to the community, armed with the advanced skills and professional ethics necessary for the humane nursing profession.
