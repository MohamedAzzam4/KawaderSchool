Admission Requirements

What do you need to join Kawader?

  • Obtaining the preparatory stage completion certificate for the current academic year.
  • Obtaining the minimum total score determined by the Ministry and the school (Nursing Coordination).
  • Passing the comprehensive medical examination (Internal, Ophthalmology, Physical Fitness).
  • Passing the personal interview, aptitude tests, and committee evaluation.
  • Applicant's age must not exceed 18 years on October 1st of the admission year.
  • Student must be a resident of Beheira Governorate (or neighboring governorates according to coordination).
