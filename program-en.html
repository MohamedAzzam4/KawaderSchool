Program Structure

Nursing Diploma Program — 5 Years

A comprehensive educational journey combining medical science study and intensive practical application.

Certified Diploma & Bright Future

Upon completing the five years, the student receives a certified nursing technician diploma from the Ministry of Health and Population, qualifying them for immediate employment in Ministry hospitals and units, or to pursue university studies in nursing colleges.

Curriculum

Your Journey Across Five Years

1

First Year

  • Basic Sciences, Anatomy & Physiology
  • General Nursing Principles
  • Biochemistry & Microbiology
  • Medical English & Terminology
2

Second Year

  • Internal & Surgical Nursing (Part I)
  • Pharmacology & Clinical Pharmacy
  • Initial Practical Training in Advanced Labs
  • Medical Psychology & Sociology
3

Third Year

  • Internal & Surgical Nursing (Part II)
  • Pediatric & Neonatal Nursing
  • Start of Actual Clinical Training in Hospitals
4

Fourth Year

  • Obstetrics & Gynecology Nursing
  • Community Health & Primary Care Nursing
  • Advanced Clinical Training (Shift System)
5

Fifth Year (Internship)

  • Comprehensive Practical Training in All Departments (ER, ICU, OR)
  • Graduation Project & Field Research
  • Quality Management & Infection Control
